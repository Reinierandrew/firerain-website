03 — How it works

## [03.headline]

Four steps. No surprises.

## [03.step1.title]

Introductory call

## [03.step1.price]

· free · 20 minutes

## [03.step1.body]

Non-binding. We talk through what you do, where the friction is, and who on your team I'd need to spend time with. We will also discuss your tech stack to find quick wins. If by the end we both think there's something here, we book step two. If not, we part ways with no invoice and no follow-up sales call.

## [03.step2.title]

On-site visit and written plan

## [03.step2.price]

· AUD 500 · 2 hours

## [03.step2.body]

I come to you, walk the floor, and sit with the people who actually do the work. Afterwards you get a written plan naming the processes worth automating, how each one would be built, and what I'd need from your end. Fixed fee, one deliverable, no surprises.

## [03.step3.title]

Pilot Automation Build

## [03.step3.price]

· quoted per job · 2 weeks

## [03.step3.body]

If the plan makes sense, I quote the first build against it. Scope is fixed before we start, the work runs two weeks, and what you get at the end is installed, tested, and handed over so your team can run it without me.

## [03.step4.title]

Build warranty, then a review

## [03.step4.price]

· included, then AUD 150/hour

## [03.step4.body]

For the first three months after go-live, if anything breaks I fix it at no charge. After that we sit down for a review, look at what's working, what needs tuning, and what to build next. Reviews and follow-on work are billed at AUD 150 per hour. No monthly retainer, no lock-in.

No monthly fee. No surprise invoices. You pay for the visit, the build, and anything you ask me to do after that. Everything in between is on me.

05 — Thirty years

## [05.headline]

Thirty years of operations. That's why this works.
